Who We Are

Built from the
inside of a board room.

GovernUp Solutions was founded in October 2025 by an active HOA board president who saw the operational and representation gaps in community management firsthand — and built the infrastructure to do it differently.

The Origin
Built from inside the boardroom
GovernUp exists because the founder sat in the board president's chair, experienced the governance gaps of the existing market firsthand, and built Covenant in response to lived frustration — not a pitch deck.
The Model
Platform and services — together
GovernUp is not a software company that decided to manage communities, or a management company that added a portal. It is both — purpose-built to reinforce each other at every level of the community governance experience.
The Vision
Local proof. Regional reach. National standard.
Starting in Prince George's County, GovernUp is building toward becoming the operating standard for transparent, technology-enabled community governance across Maryland, the DMV, and beyond.
